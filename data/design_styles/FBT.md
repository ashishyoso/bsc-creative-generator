# FBT Campaign — Design Style Guide

**Product:** Full Body Trimmer (FBT) + Special Edition
**Positioning:** Premium grooming device. The FBT is BSC's flagship hardware product — every creative must communicate premium.
**Derived from:** 11 best-performing live creatives + 15 inspiration references (reviewed April 2026)
**Purpose:** Visual rules for all FBT static ad production. Designers and AI tools reference this before creating any FBT creative.

---

## Core Principle

**FBT = Premium. Dark backgrounds only. No exceptions.**

Every FBT static uses a dark gradient background. No light backgrounds, no bright colours, no cream/white, no seasonal pastels. The FBT is positioned as a premium device — the visual language must always reflect that. Even remarketing, seasonal offers, and cart-abandonment creatives stay dark.

---

## Background System

All FBT creatives use dark gradient backgrounds. The gradient direction and colour temperature create variety while maintaining the premium foundation.

### Background Mood A: Charcoal Noir (Default)

The workhorse. Used for most creatives.

- **Colours:** #0A0A0A (centre) to #1A1A1A (edges), or reverse — dark centre fading to slightly lighter charcoal at edges
- **Gradient:** Radial spotlight from behind product, or subtle vertical light streaks
- **Texture:** Smooth. No noise, no grain. Optionally add faint vertical curtain-like light rays (ref: "SE DEC REMARKETING" creative)
- **Feel:** Studio-shot, controlled, high-end electronics unboxing

**Best for:** Product hero shots, feature callouts, offer pushes, remarketing, cart abandonment

**Reference creatives:** "DROP ENDS SOON", "LOWEST PRICE ONLY ON WEBSITE", "BLACK FRIDAY SALE", "An Exclusive Discount", "FBT checklist", "FBT SE dark floating"
**Reference inspirations:** Noise Airwave XR (#2), AirPod dark (#6), Sony WH-1000XM6 (#7), boAt headphones (#9), Gaming Headphones (#12)

---

### Background Mood B: Warm Dark Amber

Dark base with a warm amber/orange glow. Creates energy without breaking the dark rule.

- **Colours:** #0A0A0A base with warm amber glow (#C45A20 to #E5862B at ~15-20% opacity) radiating from behind/below the product
- **Gradient:** Warm light source from below or behind. The ambient glow tints the surface the product sits on, NOT the entire background
- **Texture:** Smooth dark with warm reflected light on surfaces. Can include subtle organic shapes or soft blobs in the warm zone
- **Feel:** Bold, high-energy, but still unmistakably dark and premium

**Best for:** Provocative/attention-grabbing creatives ("BEARD-BODY-BALLS"), launch moments, sale openers, scroll-stop TOF
**Reference creatives:** "BEARD BODY BALLS", "1080x1920 SE showcase"
**Reference inspirations:** JeteX Gamepad (#1 — orange ambient glow from below on dark base), Earbuds warm (#8 — dark sandy amber tone)

---

### Background Mood C: Deep Blue-Black

Dark with a cool blue undertone. Tech-forward, used for price events and spec-heavy creatives.

- **Colours:** #0A0E1A to #0F1B35. Deep navy-black, not bright blue — the blue is felt more than seen
- **Gradient:** Subtle blue-tinted radial glow behind product, or faint neon edge-light on product
- **Texture:** Can include faint geometric light grids, glowing pedestal, or subtle particle effects
- **Feel:** Tech showcase, digital, futuristic but grounded

**Best for:** Price-drop events, spec/feature-dense layouts, "biggest ever" moments
**Reference creatives:** "BIGGEST PRICE DROP"
**Reference inspirations:** AirDots PRO (#10 — dark teal with glow pedestal), Soocas toothbrush (#3 — dark navy with water dynamics)

## Product Presentation

### The Hero Rules (Non-Negotiable)

The FBT is always the hero. No models. No lifestyle. Product only.

1. **LED display showing "99"** — full charge, tech-forward signal. Must glow.
2. **Chrome/metallic finish** catching light — the primary premium cue. Rim light must create visible metallic sheen.
3. **Orange accent ring** at the base of the trimmer head — visible, not colour-corrected away.
4. **BSC logo on trimmer body** — legible but not the focal point.
5. **Magnetic attachment heads** — shown separately. Never hidden inside the case.
6. **Travel case** — present in most creatives as a value anchor. BSC logo on case visible.

### Product Staging Techniques

Derived from best performers + inspirations. Mix and rotate these across creatives:

| Technique | Description | When to Use |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| **Floating Exploded** | Trimmer centre, attachment heads hovering around it in mid-air with subtle shadows/glow | Feature callout creatives, "5 magnetic heads" messaging |
| **Lineup** | Trimmer upright, attachments standing in a row at the base, case behind | Kit/value creatives, "everything you get" |
| **Reflective Surface** | Product resting on a dark reflective plane, mirror reflection visible below | Spec-heavy creatives, premium hero shots (ref: Noise Airwave #2, earbuds #8) |
| **Pedestal / Platform** | Product elevated on a dark stone, glass, or glowing platform | Launch creatives, "new arrival" energy (ref: iPhone #4, Gaming Headphones #12, AirDots #10) |
| **In-Hand** | Product held in an open palm, attachments floating above | Provocative creatives ("BEARD-BODY-BALLS"), scale demonstration |
| **Ghost Text Behind** | Large, semi-transparent text (product name or category) sits behind the product as a watermark | Brand awareness, hero shots (ref: AirPod #6, boAt #9, AirDots #10) |

### Product Scale
- **9:16:** Product occupies 45-60% of vertical frame height
- **16:9:** Product occupies 50-65% of the right half
- **1:1:** Product occupies 50-60% of frame height, centred

## Feature Callout Styles

Rotate between these styles to keep creatives fresh. Never mix styles in the same creative.

### Style 1: Floating Chip Badges
Rounded-corner pills with semi-transparent dark fill, white text. Float around the product, positioned near the relevant part. Max 4-5 badges.
**Ref:** "1080x1920 SE showcase", "FBT SE dark floating"

### Style 2: Connector Lines
Thin lines extending from product parts to labelled callout text. Clean, technical, Apple-style.
**Ref:** iPhone 17 Pro Max (#4), AirPods Pro (#13)

### Style 3: Toggle/Checklist
iOS-style green toggle switches next to feature names, stacked vertically beside the product.
**Ref:** "FBT checklist" (Body Grooming / Beard Styling / Nose-Ears Trim)

### Style 4: Stat Numbers
Large, bold numbers (e.g., "5", "99", "11") with smaller descriptor text beside them. Scattered around the product.
**Ref:** Soocas toothbrush (#3), AirDots PRO (#10)

### Style 5: Icon Bar
Horizontal row of icon + label pairs along the bottom edge. 3-4 features max.
**Ref:** JeteX Gamepad (#1), Gaming Headphones (#12)

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight | Case | Colour |
|------|------|--------|------|--------|
| **Headline** | Elza Condensed Black (or equivalent bold condensed sans) | Black / Heavy | UPPERCASE | White (#FFFFFF) |
| **Sub-headline / badge** | Aktiv Grotesk | Bold | Title or UPPERCASE | Yellow (#FDC818) or White |
| **Feature callout text** | Aktiv Grotesk | SemiBold | Title Case | White or White at 80% opacity |
| **Stat numbers** | Elza Condensed Black | Black | — | White or Yellow |
| **Coupon code** | Elza Condensed Black | Black | UPPERCASE | Yellow (#FDC818) on dark pill, or white on yellow pill |
| **CTA button text** | Aktiv Grotesk | Bold | UPPERCASE | White on dark border, or black on white fill |
| **Ghost/watermark text** | Elza Condensed Black | Black | UPPERCASE | White at 5-10% opacity, placed BEHIND product layer |

### Typography Rules
- Maximum **3 text elements** per creative (headline + sub-line + CTA). Feature badges and ghost text don't count.
- No sentences longer than 5 words on any single text element
- Coupon codes at **minimum 48pt equivalent** — instantly readable at thumb-scroll speed
- Ghost text (when used) must be significantly larger than the headline — it's a background element, not competing

## Colour Palette

| Swatch | Hex | Usage |
|--------|-----|-------|
| **Background — Charcoal Dark** | #0A0A0A | Primary background base |
| **Background — Charcoal Edge** | #1A1A1A – #2A2A2A | Gradient edge / subtle lift |
| **Background — Warm Glow** | #C45A20 at 15-20% opacity | Ambient warm glow behind product (Mood B only) |
| **Background — Deep Blue** | #0A0E1A – #0F1B35 | Cool tech background base (Mood C only) |
| **Accent Yellow** | #FDC818 | Badges, "SPECIAL EDITION" tags, coupon codes, emphasis. ONE yellow element per creative max. |
| **Text Primary** | #FFFFFF | All headlines and body text |
| **Text Secondary** | #FFFFFF at 60-70% opacity | Sub-copy, disclaimers, supporting text |
| **Product Orange Ring** | (from product render) | Must remain visible — do not desaturate or colour-correct |
| **Social Proof Green** | #22C55E | Checkmark / trending icons in notification widgets |
| **CTA Border** | #FFFFFF or #FDC818 | Button outlines |

### Colour DON'Ts
- No red, pink, cream, or teal backgrounds — ever
- No bright background of any kind
- No gradient that goes lighter than #2A2A2A at its brightest point (exception: warm glow in Mood B, which is a localised light source, not a background shift)
- No multi-colour gradients. One base + one directional light source max.

## Composition

### Frame Zones (9:16 / 1080x1920) — Primary Format

```
┌──────────────────────────┐
│    HEADLINE ZONE         │  Top 20-25%
│    (+ sub-badge)         │  Headline + "SPECIAL EDITION" / offer tag
│                          │
│                          │
│    PRODUCT HERO ZONE     │  Middle 50-55%
│    (+ floating features) │  Trimmer + attachments + case
│    (+ ghost text layer)  │  Ghost text sits behind product in this zone
│                          │
│                          │
│    CTA / OFFER ZONE      │  Bottom 20-25%
│    (+ social proof)      │  CTA button, coupon code, notification widget
└──────────────────────────┘
```

### Frame Zones (16:9 / 1920x1080)

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  HEADLINE + COPY (left 40%)  │ PRODUCT HERO (right 60%)  │
│  • Headline top-left         │ • Trimmer + attachments    │
│  • Sub-copy / features       │ • Case behind/beside       │
│  • CTA bottom-left           │ • Ghost text behind        │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Frame Zones (1:1 / 1080x1080)

```
┌──────────────────────────┐
│    HEADLINE               │  Top 25%
│                          │
│       PRODUCT             │  Centre 50%
│    (+ features)           │
│                          │
│       CTA                 │  Bottom 25%
└──────────────────────────┘
```

## Copy Patterns

### Headline Archetypes That Work

| Archetype | Examples | When |
|-----------|----------|------|
| **Urgency** | "DROP ENDS SOON", "LIMITED STOCK", "BEFORE IT'S SOLD OUT EVERYWHERE" | Active sale, stock pressure |
| **Offer / Price** | "LOWEST PRICE ONLY ON WEBSITE", "BIGGEST PRICE DROP", "AN EXCLUSIVE DISCOUNT JUST FOR TODAY" | Price event, flash sale |
| **Provocation** | "BEARD-BODY-BALLS" | TOF scroll-stop, zone-based messaging |
| **Remarketing** | "LEFT SOMETHING BEHIND?" | Cart abandonment (still on dark background) |
| **Product Name Hero** | "Full Body Trimmer" + "SPECIAL EDITION" badge | Awareness, new audience |

### Copy DON'Ts
- No educational copy on statics (save for video)
- No long feature descriptions or sentences
- No brand story or origin messaging
- No "Buy Now" without a reason (offer, urgency, or social proof)
- No Hinglish on statics (Hinglish is for video scripts only — statics are English)

---

## Social Proof Treatment

When using social proof on FBT statics:
- Style as **platform notification widgets** — green icon + text in a rounded pill, resembling an app notification
- Examples: "560+ People Ordered This in Last 7 Days", "BEST IN CLASS"
- Position: bottom-right quadrant on 9:16, beside CTA on 16:9
- Keep to ONE social proof element per creative — never stack multiple

## Lighting Techniques

The single biggest differentiator of premium-feeling creatives is lighting. These are the approved lighting setups:

| Technique | Description | Ref |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Rim Light** | Strong light from behind/above the product, creating a bright edge outline on the chrome body. The face of the product stays darker. | "DROP ENDS SOON", most live creatives |
| **Spotlight Glow** | Soft radial light behind the product, brighter at centre, fading to dark edges. Product silhouetted against the glow. | "FBT SE dark floating", Noise Airwave (#2) |
| **Under-Glow** | Light source from below, illuminating the bottom of the product and the surface it sits on. Top of frame stays dark. | JeteX Gamepad (#1), warm amber creatives |
| **Radial Grid** | Structured light pattern (grid, concentric circles, or rays) behind the product at low opacity. Adds depth without competing. | Sony WH-1000XM6 (#7) |
| **Reflective Plane** | Product sits on a glossy dark surface that shows a mirror reflection. The reflection itself provides the "light." | Noise Airwave (#2), Earbuds (#8) |
| **Fog / Atmosphere** | Subtle smoke or fog wisps around the base of the product. Very low opacity — a suggestion, not a cloud. | iPhone 17 Pro Max (#4), ON Nutrition (#14) |

### Lighting DON'Ts
- No flat, even lighting — every creative needs a directional light source
- No coloured lights on the product body (the chrome must read as neutral metallic)
- No lens flares or starburst effects
- Warm glow (Mood B) tints the environment, NOT the product itself

## Product Render Requirements

Every FBT render/photograph must meet these specs:

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **LED Display** | Showing "99" (battery percentage indicator), glowing blue-white. Must be legible. Note: "99" is battery %, NOT runtime. Runtime is 90 minutes. |
| **Chrome Finish** | Rim light catching metallic surface. At least one strong specular highlight visible. |
| **Orange Accent Ring** | Visible at trimmer head base. Do not desaturate. |
| **BSC Logo (on product)** | Legible on the trimmer body. Not the focal point. |
| **Attachment Heads** | Shown separately — floating, lined up, or in exploded view. Never tucked inside the case. |
| **Travel Case** | BSC logo visible. Shown open, beside, or behind the trimmer. Present in 70%+ of creatives. |
| **Render Quality** | Photorealistic or actual photography. No flat illustrations, no cartoon style, no low-poly 3D. |

## Pre-Ship Checklist

Before any FBT static is approved:

- [ ] **Background is dark?** (Mood A, B, or C — no exceptions)
- [ ] **Product is the hero?** (No models, no lifestyle imagery)
- [ ] **Max 3 text elements?** (Headline + sub + CTA. Badges/ghost text don't count)
- [ ] **Yellow accent used once only?** (One badge OR one code OR one emphasis word)
- [ ] **LED display showing "99" and glowing?**
- [ ] **Chrome finish has a visible specular highlight?**
- [ ] **Orange ring visible?**
- [ ] **Clear action in the copy?** (Offer, urgency, provocation, or social proof — not generic)
- [ ] **BSC logo minimal or absent from layout?** (Product's own logo is enough)
- [ ] **Would you stop scrolling for this?**
- [ ] **Does NOT look like a traditional ad?** (Feels like premium content, not a banner)

## Quick Reference: What NOT To Do

| Don't | Why |
|-------|-----|
| Light / white / cream backgrounds | Kills premium positioning. FBT is not a budget product. |
| Models or lifestyle shots | FBT statics are product-as-hero. Models go in video only. |
| More than 3 text elements | Cluttered = cheap. Premium = restrained. |
| Flat even lighting | No drama = no premium. Every creative needs a light direction. |
| Educational/feature-heavy copy | Statics sell on emotion, urgency, and intrigue. Features go in video or PDP. |
| Multiple yellow accents | Yellow is the spice, not the dish. One pop per creative. |
| Bright coloured backgrounds of any kind | Contradicts premium dark-only rule. Even seasonal = dark. |
| Visible BSC logo in the layout | Performance creatives strip branding. Product logo is sufficient. |

# 3 for ₹999 Campaign — Design Style Guide

**Offer:** Buy any 3 products for ₹999
**Positioning:** Insane value combo — trimmer + perfumes/skincare at an unbeatable price
**Derived from:** 8 best-performing creatives (reviewed April 2026)
**Purpose:** Visual rules for all 3-for-999 static ad production.

---

## Core Principle

**₹999 is the hero. The price IS the creative.**

Every creative must make "₹999" or "3 for ₹999" the single most prominent visual element. The value proposition is so strong that the price does the selling — the creative's job is to make the price unmissable and the offer feel urgent.

---

## Product Combination Rules

1. **Always include at least 1 trimmer** if showing products
2. **Hero combo: 1 Trimmer + 2 Perfumes** — this is the proven winner
3. **Can do standalone trimmer** — just the trimmer with the ₹999 offer
4. **Can do without products** — meme/reaction creatives with just the offer text
5. **Always use ₹ symbol** — "₹999" not "999" or "Rs 999"
6. **Value prop text: "Get 3 for ₹999"** — clear, direct, no ambiguity

## Visual Tiers

### Tier 1: Dark Product Showcase — PRIMARY (~40%)

**Use for:** Product-focused consideration ads, retargeting, feed ads

| Element | Specification |
|---------|--------------|
| **Background** | Dark charcoal to black (#0A0A0A to #1A1A1A) with warm amber/gold under-glow |
| **Products** | 3 products arranged in a group — trimmer center/tallest, 2 perfumes flanking. Products must match reference images EXACTLY. |
| **Price text** | "₹999" in massive bold gold/yellow text (#FDC818), occupying 30%+ of frame height. This is the BIGGEST element. |
| **Offer text** | "3 PRODUCTS" or "TRIMMER + 2 PERFUMES" in white condensed uppercase above the price |
| **"@" prefix** | Use "@₹999" format — the "@" is the campaign's signature style |
| **Lighting** | Warm spotlight on products, dramatic shadows, products catch golden highlights |

**Reference:** "3 PRODUCTS @999" dark creative with Power Styler + NSFW + mood perfume

### Tier 2: Bright/Fun — SECONDARY (~25%)

**Use for:** TOF awareness, seasonal hooks, Instagram feed scroll-stop

| Element | Specification |
|---------|--------------|
| **Background** | Bright gradients — cyan, pink-blue pastel, or bold single colour (bright blue, bright yellow) |
| **Products** | Same arrangement rules. Products sit on a surface or float with soft shadows |
| **Price text** | "999" in massive bold red (#E21C24) or dark blue, 3D effect allowed |
| **Headline** | Seasonal/contextual hook above: "NEW YEAR NEW DEAL", "SUMMER SALE", etc. |
| **Scrolling banner** | Repeating "BUY 3 PRODUCTS AT JUST ₹999" as a diagonal/horizontal ticker strip |
| **Energy** | Fun, inviting, accessible — NOT premium. This is a value play. |

**Reference:** "NEW YEAR NEW DEAL" pastel gradient with Veleno + trimmer + Venice

### Tier 3: Meme/Reaction — SCROLL-STOP (~25%)

**Use for:** TOF cold audience, Instagram Reels cover, high-engagement bait

| Element | Specification |
|---------|--------------|
| **Format** | Popular meme templates with offer text overlaid |
| **Text** | "TRIMMER + 2 PERFUMES @₹999" in bold red/yellow on white speech bubbles or caption areas |
| **Tone** | Humour-first. The meme carries the scroll-stop, the offer text carries the conversion. |
| **Product images** | NOT shown in memes — text-only offer |
| **Stock** | Add urgency: "and the stock is limited", "WAIT! WHAT'S THAAAATT" |

**References:** Cat delivery, monkey reaction, distracted boyfriend, corporate same picture

### Tier 4: UGC/Unboxing — TRUST (~10%)

**Use for:** BOF trust-building, "proof it's real" validation

| Element | Specification |
|---------|--------------|
| **Format** | Phone-shot style. Real box, real products, handwritten price on paper |
| **Text** | "₹999" handwritten or bold overlay. "3 @999" in yellow on the box |
| **Products visible** | Trimmer box + 2 other products in/around the delivery box |
| **Authenticity** | Must look unpolished, real, not designed. Natural lighting. |

**References:** White paper "₹999" flat-lay, cardboard box "3 @999" unboxing

## Copy Patterns

### Headlines that worked:
- **Direct offer:** "3 PRODUCTS @₹999", "TRIMMER + 2 PERFUMES @₹999"
- **Seasonal hook:** "NEW YEAR NEW DEAL — BUY 3 PRODUCTS AT JUST ₹999"
- **Meme caption:** "when you see TRIMMER + 2 PERFUMES @999 and the stock is limited"
- **UGC/authentic:** "₹999" (handwritten), "3 @999" (on box)
- **Comparison:** "1 TRIMMER FOR ₹2000" vs "1 TRIMMER & 2 PERFUMES AT ₹999"

### Copy DON'Ts:
- Never write "999" without ₹ symbol in product showcase ads
- Never make the price smaller than the headline — price IS the headline
- Never show products without specifying the combo ("what 3 products?")
- Never use premium/luxury tone — this is a VALUE play, keep it accessible

## Pre-Ship Checklist

- [ ] **Is "₹999" the biggest visual element?**
- [ ] **Is at least 1 trimmer shown (if showing products)?**
- [ ] **Does the value prop read clearly: "Get 3 for ₹999"?**
- [ ] **Are product images copied exactly from references?**
- [ ] **Is there urgency ("limited stock", "only on website")?**
- [ ] **No BSC logo in layout (product packaging logos sufficient)?**
- [ ] **Would someone scrolling understand the offer in 1 second?**
